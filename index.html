About US

Most of users in Industry today produce world class products but are dependent on traditional electricity provided by government which is not sufficient for manufacturing industry for 24 hours of operation. No matter what type of business you run, we all rely on power the same way and it is important to have the necessary backup and protection when things go wrong. Power failures pose a serious financial risk to businesses and may interrupt your operations and income if you don't have a backup plan. Hence there is always a need of alternate power backup solution. As a trusted and reliable Brand of power products, we make it our goal to bring you quality devices that you can count on.

Our Vision

SRControls and Power solution is one stop solution for all the alternative power backup products including solar panels, Online UPS, Stabilizers etc. their installations, consultancy and annual maintenance at low cost and superior quality. Our vision is to help our clients to grow and complete their operations on time and need not to face any disappointments due to power failures.

Our Mission

Since, 2014 we are providing alternative power-backup products. We will continue to serve our best to make everyday business easy and to become India's Premier provider of power backup and conditioning products, services and solutions and to become the preferred power partner for our customers.
